Questions

Frequently asked questions

Are consultations and inspections really free?

Yes. Whether it's a remodel estimate, a commercial buildout consultation, or a roof and exterior inspection after a storm — the assessment costs you nothing and comes with no obligation.

What types of projects do you take on?

Residential remodels and additions, commercial construction and buildouts, exteriors (siding, gutters, windows), roofing, and storm damage restoration. One team handles it from first walkthrough to final punch list.

Do you work with insurance claims?

Yes. For storm and property damage we document everything, meet your adjuster on site, and guide you through the claim so the restoration is covered properly.

What areas do you serve?

We're based in Tomball, TX and serve the greater Houston area plus all of Southeast and Central Texas — from Orange and Beaumont to San Antonio, Freeport to Dallas–Fort Worth, and every town in between.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes. Ironwood Construction Group LLC is fully insured, and every project is completed to manufacturer specifications and local building codes.
